# Whiskr

## Introduction
Whiskr is a mobile app for couples to connect and ask themselves and each other peculiar questions they usually don't, stepping out of their comfort zone together and exploring different levels to their relationship. It's made to learn more about each other and discover things together - not get weirded out or disguted by the things we hide from others. It's to create understanding about the things we usually don't talk about. Couples that misses the early-on spark in their relationship may wish to ignite it again, and with Whiskr, you're able to. 

## Functions
Play minigames together, ask each other questions and answer them, and participate on a daily to create long-lasting streaks. Whiskr offers things from quizes to games. Feel free to also submit suggestions for features you'd like to see!
